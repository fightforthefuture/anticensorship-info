如何绕过中国政府的互联网审查制度

How to bypass Chinese Internet censorship

中国政府采用了金盾工程，也被称为“中国的伟大的防火墙，”审查因特网和刺探公民的活动。幸运的是，可以绕过审查。一般情况下，最好的办法是使用VPN服务。

The Chinese government uses the Golden Shield Project, also known as "the great firewall of China," to censor the Internet and spy on the activity of its citizens. Fortunately it is possible to get around the censorship. Generally the best way is to use a VPN service.

一个VPN服务，通过加密，并通过第三方VPN服务器重定向它掩盖了你的网络流量。这些服务器外，中国政府的影响力，像美国，或在香港西海岸的地方。使用VPN使得它很难或不可能为你的网络活动由金盾受到审查。

A VPN service disguises your Internet traffic by encrypting and redirecting it through a third-party VPN server. These servers are outside the reach of the Chinese government, in places like the West coast of the United States, or in Hong Kong. Using a VPN makes it difficult or impossible for the Chinese government to censor your online activity.

Many VPN services are blocked by the Chinese government, but here are a few that work well:

  • ExpressVPN
  • VPN.AC
  • 12VPN

使用VPN是不是非法在中国。但是，注意避免政府审查的任何步骤可能导致敌对政府的监控和打击报复。请注意风险！

Using a VPN is not illegal in China. However, any steps taken to avoid government censorship may lead to hostile government surveillance and retaliation. Please be aware of the risks!
